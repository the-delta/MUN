FROM THE SECRETARY GENERAL

Dear Prospective Participants,
I am honored to invite you to apply to the debut edition of Shri Vaishnav Vidhyapeeth Vishwavidyalaya International Model United Nations, SVVVIMUN 2017 to be held from Friday, October 6th, to Sunday, October 8, 2017 at Shri Vaishnav Vidhyapeeth Vishwavidyalaya, Indore, Madhya Pradesh.

A Model United Nation conference is one of it’s kind and what helps to set a platform which helps to open the doors of international reconnection and association for the future generations who are ready to step in to the corridors of diplomacy and seize the moment to work towards becoming responsible leaders of the feature. The wide range of topics and committees will further ensure a uniquely engaging and thought-provoking experience for each and every delegate.

Dear Prospective Delegates, I Very Strongly recommend you to take full advantages of the opportunity brought forward by SVVVIMUN 2017. I encourage you to be creative, test the limits of current international relations, while remaining realistic, learn as much as you can from this experience and bear those lessons in mind when entrusted with real decision making power in your future careers.

SVVVIMUN is a great educational opportunity, and I sincerely hope that you will choose to take part in it. I, and my Secretariat, look forward to welcoming you all to Shri Vaishnav Vidhyapeeth Vishwavidyalaya, Indore in October, 2017, the first ever University based conference in central India,where the delegates will witness one of the great MUN conference with the theme of “Believe The Change”.

Sincerely,
Sparsh Shrivastava

Secretary General
Shri Vaishnav Vidhyapeeth Vishwavidyalaya International Model United Nations 2017.
